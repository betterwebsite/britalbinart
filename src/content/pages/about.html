About Me

Hi I'm Brit!

I believe art has the power to tell a story—one that speaks to the heart, honors faith, and connects generations. My work is deeply rooted in storytelling, creating custom pieces inspired by personal journeys and scripture. Each painting is more than art; it’s an intentional reflection of hope, meaning, and connection. Through my creative process, I help bring those stories to life in a way that feels personal and deeply significant.

The walls of your home should tell your story, not someone else`s.
