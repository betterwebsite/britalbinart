About Me

Hi I'm Brit!

I believe art has the power to tell a story—one that speaks to the heart, honors faith, and connects generations. My work is deeply rooted in storytelling, creating custom pieces inspired by personal journeys and scripture. Each painting is more than art; it’s an intentional reflection of hope, meaning, and connection. Through my creative process, I help bring those stories to life in a way that feels personal and deeply significant.

The walls of your home should tell your story, not someone else`s.

Custom Artwork

Collections and Custom

My artwork is created in thoughtfully curated collections, each rooted in a deeper story and message. The "Found" Collection speaks to renewal, redemption, and the beauty of being seen and known, while the "Freedom" Collection captures themes of breakthrough, courage, and spiritual transformation.

Custom pieces are designed to tell your unique story through art, blending faith, personal memories, and meaningful details into a one-of-a-kind creation. Each piece is crafted with intention, whether inspired by a favorite scripture, a family legacy, or a deeply personal moment. My process is collaborative, ensuring that every brushstroke reflects the heart and vision behind your story.
